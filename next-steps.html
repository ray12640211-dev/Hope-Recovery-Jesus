Next Steps in Following Jesus

“If anyone desires to come after Me, let him deny himself, take up his cross daily, and follow Me.” — Luke 9:23

Armor Up for Spiritual Warfare

The Christian life is not passive. Scripture teaches we wrestle not against flesh and blood, but against spiritual forces of darkness. God provides armor so you can stand firm.

“Put on the whole armor of God, that you may be able to stand against the wiles of the devil.” — Ephesians 6:11

Deny Self and Follow Jesus Daily

Following Christ is surrender. It is not self-help. It is a new life where Jesus is Lord, not self.

“And whoever does not bear his cross and come after Me cannot be My disciple.” — Luke 14:27

Priority: An Obedient Lifestyle in Jesus

We are saved by grace through faith (not works), but obedience is the fruit of a true walk with Christ.

“If you love Me, keep My commandments.” — John 14:15

5 Things to Maximize Spiritual Growth

  1. Read daily — let the Word renew your mind.
  2. Pray daily — depend on God, not yourself.
  3. Worship in song — set your heart on Christ.
  4. Fellowship with believers — don’t walk alone.
  5. Serve others — live like Jesus, not self.

Live for God, not self.

Live for God — Not Self

“I have been crucified with Christ; it is no longer I who live, but Christ lives in me; and the life which I now live in the flesh I live by faith in the Son of God, who loved me and gave Himself for me.” — Galatians 2:20
